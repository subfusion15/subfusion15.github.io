Privacy Policy for Drive Exchange Server

Effective date: October 2025

Overview

Drive Exchange Server is a private tool designed to upload and organize scanned receipts in your Google Drive for personal tax purposes. The app uses Google OAuth for authentication and does not collect or share personal information.

Data Access

This app requests access to your Google Drive (drive.file) scope, which allows uploading and managing files created by this app only. No other files in your Drive are accessed or modified.

Data Storage

No data is stored on external servers. All access tokens and metadata remain securely managed by Google OAuth.

Sharing and Retention

We do not share, sell, or retain any user information or file data.
